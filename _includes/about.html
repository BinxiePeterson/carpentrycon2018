About

  • What are the Carpentries?

    Software and Data Carpentry are sibling organizations who teach foundational computational and data science skills to researchers.

  • What is a Workshop?

    A Software or Data Carpentry workshop comprises two full days. The instructor live codes and learners follow along. Workshops can be any size.

    Workshops are run by a team. Ideally this will consist of:

    • A host (who is the principal local contact for the workshop)
    • Certified instructors (who present the material and lead the practical exercises)
    • A lead instructor (who is in charge of deciding what will be taught by whom and ensuring the teaching aspects of the workshop go smoothly)
    • Helpers (who provide assistance during practical sessions).

  • How are Software and Data Carpentry Workshops Managed?

    Software and Data Carpentry are taught by volunteers.
